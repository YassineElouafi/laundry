# Rinse Platform Workflow Analysis

## Overview

Rinse is a technology-enabled laundry and dry-cleaning platform that connects customers with local laundry and dry-cleaning partners through a centralized logistics and customer experience layer.

## Business Model

Customer → Rinse App/Website → Driver (Valet) → Laundry Partner → Quality Control → Driver → Customer

## Customer Workflow

### 1. Account Creation
- Create account
- Add address
- Add payment method
- Select service (Wash & Fold, Dry Cleaning, Launder & Press, Hang Dry, Subscription)

### 2. Schedule Pickup
- Select pickup date/time
- Driver assigned
- ETA and notifications sent

### 3. Pickup Process
- Driver scans order
- Collects laundry
- Takes photos if needed
- Confirms pickup

### 4. Routing & Sorting
#### Wash & Fold
Pickup → Laundry Partner → Wash → Dry → Fold → Package

#### Dry Cleaning
Pickup → Inventory → Photo Documentation → Dry Cleaner → Pressing → Hanging → Package

### 5. Inventory Management
- Garment tagging
- Item counting
- Photo documentation
- Customer inventory reporting

### 6. Cleaning Operations
- Washing
- Drying
- Folding
- Stain treatment
- Dry cleaning
- Pressing

### 7. Quality Control
- Item count verification
- Packaging inspection
- Missing item detection
- Delivery readiness check

### 8. Delivery
- ETA notifications
- Driver tracking
- Doorstep or concierge delivery
